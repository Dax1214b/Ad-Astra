Understanding the Three-Body Problem

The three-body problem is one of physics' most intriguing challenges: predicting the motion of three celestial bodies interacting through gravitational forces. This fundamental question has not only pushed the boundaries of mathematics and physics but has also inspired groundbreaking scientific research and cultural works.
