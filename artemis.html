Artemis Program

Returning humanity to the Moon and beyond

Mission Overview

In Progress

Moon to Mars

The Artemis program will land the first woman and first person of color on the Moon, establishing sustainable lunar exploration as a stepping stone to future Mars missions.

First Launch: November 16, 2022 Crew Landing: 2025 Destination: Lunar South Pole

Primary Objectives

Lunar Exploration

Establish sustainable presence on the Moon with the Artemis Base Camp.

Space Technology

Develop and test new technologies for deep space exploration and Mars missions.

Scientific Research

Conduct groundbreaking lunar science and prepare for future Mars exploration.

Key Systems

Space Launch System

The most powerful rocket ever built, capable of sending crew and cargo to the Moon.

Orion Spacecraft

Advanced crew vehicle designed to carry astronauts to deep space and back safely.

Human Landing System

SpaceX Starship variant modified to land crew on the lunar surface.
